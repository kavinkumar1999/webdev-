About Us

We have more than 30 practices and 300 allied health professionals across the country. Whether you’re an elite athlete or hoping to alleviate every day pain, we can support each step of your journey towards improved and injury-free movement.


At Robinhood Care, we have some of India’s best healthcare practitioners as colleagues, leaders, and in clinic experts. Our knowledgeable team includes Specialist Sport and Musculoskeletal physiotherapists, APA titled physiotherapists, physiotherapists with post-graduate training in continence and women’s health, accredited lymphoedema therapists, and physios with skills in chronic pain management, headaches, and migraines.
